More About Chartreuse

Chartreuse is a French liqueur that comes in yellow and green varieties. It's known for its vibrant color, herbal taste, and centuries-old recipe.Carthusian monks have been making Chartreuse since 1737. The recipe is based on a secret 1605 recipe for an “elixir for long life” created by a medieval alchemist.

Taste

  • Yellow Chartreuse: Sweeter than Green Chartreuse, with notes of honey, saffron, and anise.
  • Green Chartreuse: Features prominent flavors of lime, citrus spice, and fresh-cut herbs.
